Rocky Point Survival

After a long hike up to the summit of Rocky Point Mountain, you pitch camp for the night.
But when you awake in the morning, there's a zombie outbreak!
Join other survivors and attempt to survive this zombie outbreak in a turn by turn style game!
Select your character and select a space to move or a zombie to shoot. Be careful, you could miss your shot!
Good luck!

Game by Alex Pogue, Jacob Smith and Carter Thayer

Able to be loaded on any GBA Emulator!
Recommened GBA.js: http://jpfau.github.io/gbajs/

Controls:
A - Select/Move/Shoot
B - Deselect
Start - End turn
